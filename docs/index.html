Key practices

1

Workflow driven design approach

User interviews and quantitative research is the most valuable when embeded into several stages of product discovery phase.

I always bring pivotal findings back to the execution team to grow their knowledge of product and target user base.

2

Less handoff documentation, more collaboration

Tight collaboration with engineering helps me make accurate timeline estimates.

Fluid product roadmap is an artifact of constant improvement.

3

Deliver short term while playing long term

Constant alignment with product strategy informs short term product goals and engeneering focus.

Planning for tech debt housekeeping as a part of a delivery can save $$$ developing future projects.
